=================================================
Case Study 1: First Steps into the Izenda World
=================================================

This case study will guide a novice user through the major themes of Izenda. Proceeding modules may contain reports parts, reports, dashboards, and settings created in previous modules. Nevertheless, each module can stand alone as a useful learning tool. To follow this tutorial, request for access to a Northwind Database instance (https://northwinddatabase.codeplex.com/) from you database administrator.
